I am a sophomore at Peking University, majoring in Artificial Intelligence. I joined the Tong Class (an AGI program founded by Prof. Song-Chun Zhu), being a member and the monitor in the class of 2021.

My research focuses on cognitive reasoning and machine learning. I'm also interested in natural language processing, character animation and developmental psychology. In 2022 summer, I researched on social intelligence in nonverbal signals via physically grounded simulation under the guidance of Prof. Yujia Peng and Dr. Lifeng Fan at Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence (BIGAI).

In my spare time, I love dancing, baking and watching documentaries. These hobbies give me an opportunity to relax thoroughly, explore heartily and create freely.
